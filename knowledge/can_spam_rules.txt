# CAN-SPAM Compliance Rules (Federal Trade Commission)
# EVERY outgoing email MUST comply with these rules.
# Violations carry penalties of up to $53,088 PER email.

## The 8 Requirements:

1. NO FALSE OR MISLEADING HEADERS
   The "From", "To", "Reply-To", and routing information must be accurate
   and identify the person who initiated the message.

2. NO DECEPTIVE SUBJECT LINES
   The subject line must accurately reflect the content of the email.
   Do NOT use clickbait, misleading urgency, or fake "Re:" prefixes.

3. IDENTIFY THE MESSAGE AS AN AD (if applicable)
   For internship outreach, this is less strict since you're not selling
   a product, but be transparent about your intent.

4. INCLUDE YOUR PHYSICAL POSTAL ADDRESS
   For personal outreach, your city and state are often sufficient.
   (Note: for an internship cold email, including your university name
   and location is generally acceptable.)

5. PROVIDE AN OPT-OUT MECHANISM
   Every email must include a clear way for the recipient to stop
   receiving emails. For cold outreach, a simple line like:
   "If you'd prefer not to receive emails from me, just reply 'unsubscribe'
   and I'll remove you from my list immediately."

6. HONOR OPT-OUTS WITHIN 10 BUSINESS DAYS
   If someone asks to stop receiving emails, you MUST stop.
   Our system tracks this in the database (status = 'opted_out').

7. MONITOR WHAT IS DONE ON YOUR BEHALF
   Since the AI agent sends emails on your behalf, YOU are responsible
   for ensuring compliance. Always review emails before sending.

8. EACH EMAIL MUST CONTAIN:
   - Your real full name
   - Your university / affiliation
   - An opt-out line
   - An honest subject line
   - Accurate sender information
